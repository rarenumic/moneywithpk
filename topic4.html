Asset Allocation: The Cornerstone of Portfolio Design

Asset allocation is how you divide your portfolio among equity, debt, cash, and other asset classes. It has a greater impact on long-term returns than individual stock picking.

Proper allocation reflects your goals, time horizon, and risk tolerance—and it evolves as your life circumstances change.
