Building an Emergency Fund: Your First Investment Step

An emergency fund is 3–6 months of expenses set aside in a liquid account to handle life’s surprises. It’s not an investment for returns—it’s an investment in peace of mind.

Build it before you start equity investing. It ensures you never withdraw from long-term investments for short-term needs.
