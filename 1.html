Understanding Tax-Efficient Investing

Tax-efficient investing involves strategies that minimize the tax impact on your investment returns. This can include using tax-advantaged accounts, harvesting losses, and selecting investments with favorable tax treatment.

For long-term wealth management, understanding how taxes affect your portfolio is critical to maximizing net returns.
